# Dune: Awakening SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 6 clicks, 3,863 impressions, 0.16% CTR, average position 37.73. The homepage lacked a canonical URL and legacy `newsdune` queries generated 1,035 unrelated impressions.

## 0–30 days

- Correct canonical, Open Graph and social descriptions; remove stale “Coming in 2025” wording.
- Add visible fan-site identity and `WebSite`/`VideoGame` structured data that does not imply official ownership.
- Inspect GSC URL inspection, links and indexed pages for `newsdune`; redirect or remove only the specific legacy URL if one exists. Do not create content for the unrelated query.

## 31–60 days

- Rebuild the news hub around verified official announcements, with a source URL, date and last-checked date on each entry.
- Prioritize original guides for survival, crafting, bases, classes and updates rather than thin news summaries.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Add a weekly official-source review and revise pages only when primary sources change.
- Move homepage/news into the top 20 and get five verified high-intent guide pages into the top 30.
