Frequently Asked Questions

Where should I verify current availability and release information?
Use Funcom and the official storefront rather than an undated article. This fan guide records source links and review boundaries, but store availability can change by region and platform.
How do I check my platform and PC requirements?
Open the official product listing for the platform you plan to use, then compare the current operating system, processor, memory, graphics, and storage requirements one by one.
Can I rely on an older guide after an update?
Treat an older guide as a planning reference, not a permanent rule. Confirm its patch or source date, then test one changed setting, route, or decision at a time.
Where can I find guides without unverified map or build claims?
Begin with the survival, base-planning, character, and requirements pages on this site. Each guide separates planning advice from facts that require a current primary source.
