Dune: Awakening Product & Platform Guide

Use this page to make a safer store decision: verify the live listing first, compare only like-for-like editions and platforms, and keep a date beside any detail that can change.

1. Start With the Live Listing

Open the official Funcom page and the storefront for the platform and region you actually intend to use. A copied launch article can be wrong about availability, account scope, price, or edition contents even when its title looks current.

  • Check that the listing matches your platform.
  • Check the selected region and currency.
  • Read the current account and network notes.

2. Compare Editions by Included Value

Make a small list of what each edition explicitly includes on the current official listing. Do not assume a bonus, soundtrack, cosmetic item, early-access label, or upgrade path is universal across platforms or remains available after a promotion changes.

  • Record the source URL and review date.
  • Compare contents before price.
  • Keep promotional wording separate from confirmed items.

3. Check PC Requirements Separately

Minimum and recommended requirements are not a single score. Compare operating system, processor, memory, graphics, available storage, and connection requirements one by one. Leave installation headroom and use the system requirements guide for a safe comparison order.

  • Do not copy a requirements list without a date.
  • Check whether your graphics memory is comparable.
  • Test one setting at a time after installation.

4. Keep a Purchase Note

Before buying, write down the platform, region, edition, price, and source date you checked. This simple note makes it clear whether a later question comes from a changed store offer, a different edition, or an older article.
